What is Zombie Horde?

Zombie Horde is a server plugin for Counter-Strike: Source that turns a standard CS:S server into a cooperative survival shooter. A team of humans must work together to survive a horde of zombies — only headshots count. Developed 2005–2007 by .robot & electronaut (Australian open-source project).

Gameplay

  • 32-player server recommended — 18 bots play as zombies
  • Zombies receive a health boost and self-healing on spawn
  • Only direct headshots permanently kill a zombie
  • Various zombie classes with different abilities
  • Zombies drop green eggs — powerups for humans (watch out: poison!)

Powerups (13 Types)

  • Berserk • Speed • Infinite Ammo • Low Gravity • Slow Motion
  • Health Regen • Assault Suit • Teleport • Full Health • Full Ammo
  • Quad Damage • Zombification • Ratsack (poison!)

Admin Commands (Selection)

  • zh_ac — open anti-camp menu
  • zh_restrict / zh_unrestrict — restrict / unrestrict weapons
  • zh_resetrank / zh_resetallranks — reset player rank(s)
  • zh_powerup — manually spawn a powerup
  • zh_incslot / zh_decslot — adjust zombie slot count
