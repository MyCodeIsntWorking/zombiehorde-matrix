Server Information

Game Mode

  • Players play exclusively as CT (Counter-Terrorists)
  • Enemies are Zombies (Team T)
  • Only Headshots deal permanent damage to Zombies
  • Zombies respawn once at the same spot where they died — Watch out!

Zombie Info

Number of Zombies16
DifficultyExpert
HP per Zombie650
Respawn1× at the death position
Respawn HP325
Zombie TypesZombie (red/white) · Corpse (blue) · Charple (thin/black) · Ghost (transparent)

Speeds: Charple → fastest  |  Corpse / Ghost → normal  |  Zombie → slightly slower

Combat Mechanics

  • HE Grenade: Zombie burns for 5 sec. + slowed for 5 sec.
  • Knife (Corpse Zombie): Hard knife attack → Fire & Slow effect on the CT-Player
  • Damage Display: Damage dealt is briefly shown after each hit
  • Restricted Weapons: G3SG1, SG550, SG552, Dual Elites, M249, Flashbang, Smoke, Defuser
